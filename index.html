How It Works

1

Create a Room

Click on the 'create new room' button and enter a username of your choice.

2

Copy Room ID

Copy the room ID by clicking on the 'Copy ROOM ID' button.

3

Join as Another User

Open another browser/browser-window or an incognito tab and go to http://localhost:3000.

4

Enter Room ID

Enter the same room ID to join the collaborative session.
